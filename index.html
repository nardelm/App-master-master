There are many features included in myUConn Stamford from accessing emils and phone numbers of faculty, staff and students to LIVE tracking of buses and browsing diningmenus. Explore of what my UConn Stamford has to offer by reading the full list of features.

The Official UConn Stamford Mobile App

A simple and beautiful way to stay connected.

Find where you need to go easily by picking a location/building from the menu and seeing where it is on the interactive map. You can also pinpoint your exact location with the GEO Locator.

Search and browse for emails, phone numbers and mailing addresses of university, faculty, staff and students. Save recent searches, add people directly to your contancts, or do a reverse lookup by phone.

Check out the latest events from the main UConn events calendar. Set which calenders you want to deed you events list or drill down by individual calendars. Customize your experience in the settings tab.
